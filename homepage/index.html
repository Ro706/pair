About Us

ePathshaala is an AI-powered software designed to assist students in selecting the best teacher or learning resources for specific topics and facilitate personalized learning journeys. ePathshaala provides tailored recommendations, fosters effective learning, and helps students achieve their academic goals.

    Key Features:

  • Topic-based Teacher Matching.
  • Learning Resource Suggestions.
  • Personalized Study Plans.
  • Real-time Progress Tracking
  • User Profiling and Preferences
